Prof. Albert Rakoto ratsimamanga (** : https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC3001617/)

Ambassador to France, appointed June I960. Outstanding scientist in the field of biology and a distinguished diplomat, 
serving his country in France since independence and also holding the appointments of Ambassador 
to Russia and to China on a non-residential basis. A descendant of the former royal family, 
he settled in France as a young man and married a French woman.
